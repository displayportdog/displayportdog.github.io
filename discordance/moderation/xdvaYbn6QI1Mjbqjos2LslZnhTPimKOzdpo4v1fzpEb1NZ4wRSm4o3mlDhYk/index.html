Jenson has been developed with the intention of being as straight-forward as possible.

Unfortunately, however, this isn't always the case. As commands are added and updated, things tend to get a bit messy, so if Jenson's behaviour has you stumped, you should be able to figure out what's going on through here.

If you ever have any ideas on how Jenson could be improved, please put it in #engineers-comm, and I'll do my best to implement your feedback.
