Coding Jenson:

dpdog • 27/12/2021 • ~0 words

There are probably millions of unfinished projects out there – little unpolished creations that, despite their novelty and charm, never got to see the light of day. We're all guilty of it, aren't we? How often have you started something, seeking to challenge yourself in the pursuit of new skill and knowledge, only to call it quits a few months, weeks, or even days later?

I'm thoroughly pleased to say that Jenson has not yet fallen victim to such a fate; tinkering around with some code and being able to instantly get community feedback has been absolutely wonderful for a novice such as myself. Having been able to enjoy my hobby the last few weeks whilst simultaneously providing a service for my favourite community has made me happier than ever, and I want nothing more than to give you an insight into what exactly I've been doing this whole time.
