Here are some things that Jenson can do:

help

Display all available commands. Probably how you ended up here.

gm, ga, ge, gn

Greetings and farewells! Jenson has a bunch of prefabricated messages, one of which will be randomly selected and sent when you use this command.

sprint

This command is essentially a timer - jenson supports a duration in seconds, minutes, or hours, for example '!sprint 5h' or '!sprint 2m' will run for five hours or two minutes respectively. Decimal values are supported, albeit not recommended. You can optionally append a message to the end of your timer (e.g. '!sprint 2h Don't leave the oven on, @dpdog!', but remember to tag yourself again.

whois

This is one of the first commands ever implemented, and so is due an update. You can tag a user to get basic info on them. Use it like '!whois @dpdog'

guildinfo

Get information about the current guild! This doesn't need or even accept any arguments.

meow/woof/screech

This one grabs a random cat (!meow), dog (!woof), or fox (!screech) picture. They're all cute, but they're not mine, unfortunately.

gif

This one just searches for gifs. '!gif sad'. Go wild.

define

Defines your word, and gives you a bunch of synonyms. Doesn't really work too well with conjugated verbs, but will still give you a cambridge link. '!define word'

synonymise

Shows synonyms for your word. Exactly the same as define, just a little bit quicker if you don't want a definition.

urban

Defines your word, but on urban dictionary. 98% chance of something weird or gross coming out of that one - not my fault!

lvl

Shows your current level in a little level card. Cute! Tag other users to get theirs - e.g. '!lvl @dpdog'

leaderboard

Shows the server XP leaderboard. Will display top 20 by default, add a number on the end to go through pages.

coinflip

Flip a pound coin! Returns heads, tails, or if you're very unlucky, the coin's edge.

choose/roll

This one requires arguments - it picks an item out of a list of items. For example '!choose red, green, blue, yellow' could return red, but also any of the other three options. The neat part is you don't know which one it'll spit out.

poll

Create a poll! You can simply pose a yes or no question (e.g. '!poll Movie night tonight?') or add options (e.g. '!poll Movie night? | tonight | wednesday | thursday | not feeling it').

yeet

Send a user to be productive. Sends a prefabricated embed. '!yeet @dpdog'

yeetsleep

Force your friends to sleep! It's being cruel to be kind, isn't it? '!yeetsleep @dpdog'

prompt

Get inspired! Gets a random prompt from /r/writingprompts.

cookie

Gives a virtual cookie to the tagged user. This isn't tallied, it's just fun. '!cookie @dpdog'

hug

Everyone deserves love! Hug the recipient. This is tallied, and your hug counter will increase each time! '!hug @dpdog'

bday

Displays your birthday, as far as the bot knows, and tells you if it's today (though I think you should really know)

bday-set

Set your birthday. You should include the timezone that your country is in on your birthday, as a difference from UTC. e.g. 'bday-set 04/10 +1' would set your birthday to the 4th of October, in UTC+1 (UK Daylight Savings)

bday-upcoming

Show the next ten upcoming birthdays.

suggest

Suggest something! e.g. '!suggest memes channel'
