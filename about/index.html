About me

dpdog • 25/12/2021 • ~250 words (quick read)

For a while now, I've put aside making any sort of place to journal my thoughts. And I cannot stress just how much I regret that – not because I think that the world could have used the weird and mundane things that come out of my mind, but because now I've forgotten those things. What a shame.

That's why I've put together this site. I'm no web designer, or writer, or programmer, or actually anything, now that I think of it – but I still want a little corner of the internet that I can call mine.

So here I present to you the dpblog, partly because I have no desire to attach my real name to the silly little ramblings that will undoubtedly slowly appear on here, but mostly because it was a pun, and I like that quite a lot.

I'm not terribly active on social media, so if you're looking to spend hours digging through old tweets, I'm afraid you'll be as disappointed as I was when England lost the world cup - i.e. You couldn't care less, but will pretend to, because everyone around you seems to.

You'll probably end up seeing snippets of coding or writing, and while I don't excel at either, I enjoy both, so hopefully the good vibes are enough to keep you here!

I also love to photograph anything and everything, so enjoy the mundane cover photos that come with each post.
